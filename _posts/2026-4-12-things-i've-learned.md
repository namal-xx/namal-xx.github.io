# **From zero Python to a deployed ML app, things I learned along the way**

No bootcamp, no degree, just projects, bugs, and lessons learned the hard way.

---


I had zero programming experience when I started learning ML. It was difficult, I didn’t know what’s happening, what I’m supposed to do. Everything was so confusing. No one around me could help me. I was completely lost.

The only thing I knew for sure was that no matter how difficult it is, I’ll do it. I’ll spend as much time as it takes, but I have to do it. Because there’s no other way. There’s no way I’m leaving everything behind. I have to keep going.

There were nights when I genuinely felt so demotivated because I couldn’t understand that concept, couldn’t solve that error. I used to think programming is not for me.

But after months of learning, debugging, and those restlessness nights, I finally created my own AI-Driven CRM insights app. It is the very first project I’ve spent 2 months on, and there are many things I’ve learned along my journey, and I want to share it with people who’re at the same stage as I was in.
